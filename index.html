Unique Selling Points

Inspired by a Miniclip game called Bloxorz. But instead of using a fixed 2x1 block, we start with a single 1x1 cube, which can attach to other cubes forming more complex shapes. We also introduce various environment settings such as ice, doors and bridges. The strategies of problem solving in this game can be varied. Core game mechanics are unique in themselves. Abstract, yet unique and cute design aesthetic.

Level and Puzzle Design
